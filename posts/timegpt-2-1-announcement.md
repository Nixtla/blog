## Introducing TimeGPT 2.1

At Nixtla, our mission is to push the limits of time-series forecasting and make cutting-edge models accessible to every organization.

Today, we are excited to welcome **TimeGPT 2.1** as the latest member of the TimeGPT 2 model family, the next step in enterprise-grade forecasting performance, reliability, and flexibility.

---

## **Built for Real-World Forecasting**

TimeGPT 2.1 is a single model that supports deployments at any scale and in any environment, including on-premise and fully self-hosted setups.

With TimeGPT 2.1, organizations benefit from major new and improved capabilities:

- **Improved handling of exogenous variables** for richer, more informed forecasts
- **Support for multivariate forecasting**, enabling cross-learned forecasts
- **Reduced minimum sample requirements**, unlocking strong performance even with limited history
- **Enhanced fine-tuning**, delivering higher accuracy with fewer iterations
- **Improved feature contributions**, enabling clearer interpretability and trust
- **Guaranteed non-negative outputs**, perfect for use cases such as demand, volume, or traffic forecasting

This combination makes TimeGPT 2.1 a highly versatile and production-ready release in the TimeGPT 2 family.

---

## **Top Performance**

TimeGPT 2.1 consistently ranks among the top models on widely used industry benchmarks, including:

- **GiftEval** – multi-domain forecasting performance
- **FEV** – forecast error variance
- **VN1** – prediction accuracy across horizons

Reproducible benchmark results are available on request

---

## **Enterprise-Ready from Day One**

TimeGPT 2.1 is engineered for seamless production deployment, whether in the cloud, on-premise, or fully air-gapped environments.

Key capabilities include:

- **One-line on-premise deployment**
- **Scalable inference across millions of time series**, with support for CPU, GPU, and multi-GPU setups
- **SOC 2 readiness**, meeting the strict security and compliance requirements of enterprise teams

Combined with its modular design and enhanced feature set, TimeGPT 2.1 provides a foundation for highly accurate, interpretable, and reliable forecasting across all operational contexts.
